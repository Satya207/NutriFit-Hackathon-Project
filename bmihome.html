Quick Tips

  • Half plate veggies/salad, quarter protein, quarter smart carbs.
  • Hydration: ~30–35 ml/kg/day (adjust for weather & activity).
  • Prefer whole grains & millets; limit added sugar and ultra-processed foods.
  • Protein target: ~1.0–1.6 g/kg/day; higher for muscle gain.
  • Sleep & stress management boost your results.
